About Me

I'm a third year PhD student at National University of Singapore, working with Trevor E. Carlson as my advisor. My primary research interest is on Security and Privacy. Currently, I'm focused on Systems Security challenges in modern and general purpose processors. These challenges vary across different side-channels, like timing, power, and EM.
